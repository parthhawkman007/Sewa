🧠 Overview

Sewa App is a data-driven platform that helps NGOs and social organizations centralize community data and efficiently coordinate volunteers to solve real-world problems.

Instead of relying on scattered paper surveys and disconnected reports, Sewa transforms raw data into actionable insights and connects the right volunteers to the right tasks at the right time.

🎯 Problem Statement

Local NGOs and community groups collect important data about social issues, but:

❌ Data is scattered across multiple sources
❌ No clear visibility of urgent problems
❌ Volunteers are not efficiently utilized
❌ Manual coordination slows down impact

This leads to delayed responses and reduced effectiveness in solving community issues.

💡 Solution

Sewa App provides a centralized digital ecosystem that:

📊 Collects and organizes community data
🔍 Identifies high-priority needs
🤝 Matches volunteers with relevant tasks
⚡ Enables faster and smarter decision-making
